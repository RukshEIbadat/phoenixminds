CBT
Thought-Feeling-Behaviour Triangle
  • Draw triangle: Think / Feel / Do
  • Use a recent situation — what did Aayan think first?
  • Name the feeling using emotion chart — do not guess
  • Explore what he did — think of 3 alternatives
  • Role-play the new thought and action 5 minutes
DBT
TIPP Skills — Emotional Regulation
  • T — Cold water on face — 30 seconds
  • I — 2 minutes jumping jacks
  • P — Breathe in 4, hold 2, out 6 — repeat 5x
  • P — Progressive muscle squeeze and release
  • After regulation: use feelings card — never debrief in crisis
ABA Home
Discrete Trial Training — Parent Version
  • Choose ONE skill: e.g., Touch your nose
  • Give clear instruction — wait 3 seconds
  • Correct: immediate praise + preferred reward
  • Incorrect: guide physically — then reward
  • 10 trials, twice daily, 5 days/week — log each
Social Stories
Personalised Narrative — AI Generated
  • Phoenix Synap generates story using Aayan's name
  • Preferred characters and uploaded photo used
  • Read slowly — 1 page per minute
  • After: ask 2 questions — explore, do not quiz
  • Same story 5 days before real-life exposure
Play Therapy
Child-Directed Play — 30 Minutes Daily
  • Child chooses the activity entirely
  • Describe what you observe, not direct
  • Reflect feelings: You seem happy when that falls
  • Set only safety limits — permit everything else
  • No phone. 30 full minutes undivided.
